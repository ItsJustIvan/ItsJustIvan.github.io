Ideation:

Scriptwriting is definately one of my weaker skills.
After spending most of Monday & Tuesday coming up with the story beats, and concepting out:

  • Hook
  • Intro
  • Who I Am
  • What I Do
  • Outro

I decided the tone of the piece was to double down on my strengths, which is I will never be better at any vertical catagory than other candidates, but I can offer a 1-man media swiss army knife. With that in mind, I was ready to go into Pre-Production.

Process Breakdown:

Pre-Production Production Post-Production Project Managment
Script breakdown is my bread and butter.
Wednesday evening was spend just breakding down the animation scenes and fleshing out the timing to answer:
Who I am?
What I do?
Why Hire me?
Not an excuse, but I am currently in the middle of packing for a move.
My camera and gear is about 300 miles away, I still have my fulltime job, and I was just running a webinar and have on-demand content to crank out.
I've been sitting and mulling over the script all Thursday, but after squeezing out an hour before dinner on Friday I sat myself in front of a GoPro, and just tried to get usable footage.
Wrapped shooting in the evening Friday and immediate went to do a rough cut to the script's specifications
I thought I was on a good pace, and decided to proceed with gathering B-roll, and start some of the simple motion graphics.
Ended up spending more time than anticipated, and finished all the animation, and overlays to convey the message by Saturday.
Proceeded to finish audio touch up, and render.
I didn't forget, the purpose of this exercise is not the video asset, but the project file. Sunday was spend collecting the media assets, and zipping the file for final delivery.
Additionally, I rendered the video for final inspection, and decided the best way to deliver was to build this whole landing page, for you to experience the video concept for yourself.

Et Cetera:

Victim of Circumstance: I am currently preparing for a move, so my equipment is currently ~300 miles away from me.
Being without my camera, lights, and other gear, I was relying primarily on motion graphics, stock video, and my old portfolio of content to convey the message of "Why Hire Ivan."
