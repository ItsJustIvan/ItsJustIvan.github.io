A 2 day summit for security thought leadership

Background:

After growing the Thinkcurity hub to an average monthly traffic of 25k sessions, and a year of planning. Our team wants to execute on a 2 day summit, which was the cumulation of the Blogs, Articles, Tech review, Monthly webinars, and on-Demand video content. We came up with was a 2 day live stream of Keynote speakers with leaders of the security industry, round table discussions, and technology demos for solutions faced by the industry with a live Q&A for each session.

Objective:

To run a virual even with live keynote speeches, roundtable discussions, and pre-recorder technology sessions led by security experts & thought leaders.

Process

To create the media assets for the following:

  • Promotional materials(Fackbook, Linkedin, Instagram, Email, and Banner Ads)
  • Production materials(Slides, OBSScenes, Stringer Transitions)
  • Post Event On-Demand Assets
  • Pre-recorded Speaker keynotes & Tech Seshions
  • Prep Streaming platform for Live Q&A's
  • Stinger transitions for live transitions
