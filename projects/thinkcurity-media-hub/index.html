Thinkcurity: A Media Hub for Security Professionals

Oct 5, 2020

Silvertrac Software needed a better way to reach and educate security businesses. Instead of attending the big industry events, we built our own. Thinkcurity became the go-to content hub for security insights — complete with webinars, blog articles, expert panels, and streaming content.

Services Used:
  • Go-To-Market Strategy
  • Webinar Production
  • Content Strategy
  • Video Production

## Background Silvertrac Software, a tool for security officer management, found that many of its customers — small and mid-sized security companies — couldn't justify the cost of attending major industry conferences. So instead of buying a booth at someone else's show… we became the show.

## Objective - Establish a thought leadership media platform for the physical security industry - Create a backend system that allows contributors and copywriters to easily publish content - Produce and distribute monthly webinars with industry experts - Build a brand that is educational, professional, and uniquely positioned to own the conversation

## Execution - HubSpot-powered CMS for publishing articles, videos, and lead-gen offers - A custom branding system, visual identity, and templates for all webinar & video assets - Monthly live-streamed webinars, edited into short-form content and on-demand media - Platform growth across email, social, and SEO channels — all tracked via HubSpot workflows

## Results - Consistent 4-figure attendance across live webinars - Increased inbound leads for Silvertrac Software - Established Thinkcurity as a credible source in a fragmented industry
